5. How to make and eat ramen really fast

*You only need 1$ and some skill to do this.

    2. After you bring the ramen home, just leave it on the table and get a bowl
    3. Get some hot water into the bowl, don't boil it with a pot though, because that takes a lot of time. Use your water machine with a hot mode or something.
    4. Rip out your ramen pack and add the ramen and all the spices in the packet into your bowl. Take a pair of chopsticks to mix it.
    5. Wait 10 seconds for it to be cooked. Then sit down and enjoy.
