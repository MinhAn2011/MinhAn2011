Why You Should Sleep in Class

Although sleeping in class may seem like a bad thing, it can actually be beneficial for your health and academic performance. Studies have shown that taking short naps during the day can improve memory, concentration, and productivity.

How to Sleep in Class

  1. Choose a comfortable position.
  2. Find a quiet and dark corner of the classroom. Make sure you are behind a snitch or a teacher's pet.
  3. Don't use a pillow. It causes you to snore. Use a brick or your books.
  4. Don't cover your eyes. It will make the teacher become suspicious.

Tips for Sleeping in Class

  • Don't set an alarm. The teacher will know that you are sleeping if you do that.
  • Snore a lot. This will alert the teacher but your classmates will be annoyed so it's worth it.
  • Be aware of your teacher's rules about sleeping in class.
